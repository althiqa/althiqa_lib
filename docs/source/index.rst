Welcome to althiqa's documentation!
===================================
.. image:: ./images/logoAlthiqa.png
            :align: center
            :alt: Banner
            
**althiqa** is a SaaS service to make AI evaluation simple and trustworthy. It allows data scientists, ML engineers and ML researchers to communicate easily the key aspects of their models to all stakeholders: business experts and managers, compliance and risk teams etc. althiqa is not an autoML platform but a service to facilitate ML reporting and ML communication. The users are end-to-end in full control of their metrics and their models. They interact with the SaaS service through althiqa's *simple* and *intuitive* API. You can visit `our website <https://althiqa.io>`_


.. Check out the :doc:`usage` section for further information, including how to :ref:`installation` the project.

.. note::

   This project is under active development.
